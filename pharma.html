Frequently Asked Questions

Are generic medicines as effective as branded ones?

Yes, generic medicines contain the same active ingredients as their branded counterparts and are equally effective. The difference is mainly in the inactive ingredients and packaging. All generic alternatives on PharmaRyt are doctor-verified for safety and efficacy.

How does the 30-minute delivery work?

Our 30-minute delivery is available in select cities where we have partnered with local pharmacies. When you place an order, it's routed to the nearest partner pharmacy that has your medicines in stock, and our delivery partners ensure it reaches you within 30 minutes.

Do I need a prescription to order medicines?

Yes, prescription medicines require a valid doctor's prescription to be uploaded in the app before checkout. Over-the-counter medications can be ordered without a prescription. We strictly adhere to regulatory guidelines for medicine dispensing.

How do I know the medicines are authentic?

All medicines on PharmaRyt are sourced directly from licensed pharmacies and authorized distributors. We have a rigorous quality check process, and each medicine comes with a verification code that you can check for authenticity. We also provide batch numbers and expiry dates for all products.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a no-questions-asked refund if medicines are delivered in damaged condition or are incorrect. Simply raise a complaint within 24 hours of delivery with photos of the received products, and our customer support team will process your refund or replacement.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time through the 'My Orders' section in the app. For 30-minute deliveries, you'll be able to see the delivery partner's location on a map as they approach your address.
